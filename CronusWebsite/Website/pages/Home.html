We are excited to present the web version of Manual Durations application, Cronus with enhanced features. As the legend suggests, this application aims to provide an easy interface to Trade Floor and Analytics users to analyze and modify the security risk measures produced or expected to be produced by the models in the Night Cycle. It has been designed after months of experience with Manual Duration and feedback from various quarters.

It is also expected to greatly reduce the manual effort of Operations team in handling day to day requests related to security risk measures from business users. With a great auditing process, this application endeavors to bring more transparency into the most critical component of the night cycle, risk measures.

Please write to CronusHelp@pimco.com for access to application or any other feedback. This page will be updated with the details of enhancements on the day of release as displayed below.
